WHAT IS CHILLHUB?

When the FirstBuild community started in May 2014, community members were submitting ideas for new refrigerator peripheral devices. The missing links were power and internet connectivity. Community members Brian Wagner and Nicolas Grenié designed the first-ever internet connected USB ports in a refrigerator. ChillHub opened up the refrigerator to the development of internal devices that connect effortlessly to the "FirstBuild" iOS app.

WHAT DOES IT DO?

ChillHub is a refrigerator with two seamlessly integrated USB hubs. This first-of-its-kind platform enables development of new hardware products that benefit from operating inside a cooled space.

From niche to mass market, ChillHub will allow the developer community to create new devices that could benefit all refrigerator users.

FEATURES

INTEGRATED

Provides seamless integration with the refrigerator. Allows access to sensor data and control of refrigerator components.

POWERFUL

Build-in USB power and WiFi connectivity provide common elements required for nearly any application.

OPEN

The development platform is open, allowing FirstBuild community members to share and improve upon applications.

MOBILE

The open-source, iOS-compatible app connects you to ChillHub anywhere.


DETAILED SPECS


  • 2 USB Ports
  • USB power: 5V, 4A, 20W (wire dependent)
  • WiFi connected (wireless n and earlier)
  • Software developers kit (node.js)
  • Open source iOS app
